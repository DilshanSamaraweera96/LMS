About Us

SipSewana is a Library Service Provider in Mathugama town with the objective of providing a quality service at free of charge for those who are seeking knowledge.

We continuously improved our service offering & added new services to our portfolio to provide the best of care to our members.

With the rapid growth of economy and infrastructure in the country as well as in the Kalutara district, there is a huge demand in the Library services, creating a necessity of Library system to cater to the needs of the community who are willing to and can afford to have a better service.

Our future growth plans include expanding our services as Library system throughout the western province and beyond to become a golden landmark among the Library organizations in the country.
