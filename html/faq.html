FAQS

01.What is SipSewana?

02.Why Choose SipSewana?

SipSewana is the only Library service provider in Mathugama Town.Have lots of coporative services including good support team & lot's of books to discover and read the books you like the most.

03.What are the benifits of this Website?

This is the official Website of SipSewana Library.This is 24hours service,so you can log into this any moment.You can know more about our info,our book store,our contacts through your mobile without reaching our branch.you can also order book online through this website without wasting anytime of yours.Also you cancel your order anytime with this.

GUIDE & INSTRUCTIONS

01.How to Online reserve a book?

After you enter our website,First you need to create a account.After that you can login into our website.In nav menu click "Books" tab to enter our book collection.In Books Menu You can check categories that suits for your choice and choose.Also you can search your relevant book using the search bar.
After you select the book you can refer some pages which contains the book.Click the corners inthe book cover to scroll to the next page.You cant view those if you are not logged into our website. So you MUST logged into our website.After viewing you can reserve your book by Clicking RESERVE BOOK button which is in the 2nd page.

02.What happens after the reservation?

After you clicked the RESERVE BOOK button system will check the reservation availability & inform you whether your reservation is issued or not.If the reservation success you can view your reservation in MY ACCOUNT page.You can check issued book Collect date, Return date & Fine details in this page.
Sometimes, The book that you want reserve may not available in the library at the moment.So if that happens we will store your request and inform you with Notification as soon as it available.Also it will Automatically filled in to your request.You can check it on Pending Reservation tab which is in the "My account" page.

03.How many books can I reserve?

You can hold 3 issued books at a time including pending book request. Also you cant reserve same book at the same moment. If one of those conditions are happens,Reserve Book button will disable to your account. After you reutrns the books you can reserve more books.

04.How can I Know Collectdate & Returndate?

You can check the collectdate if you refer My Account.If a book was issued to your account it will appear on issued book tab.In that tab you can check the collect according to your issued book.Normally you have to collect reserved book within 3days.If you didnt collect the book within that time your reservation will be cancelled.You can also cancel the reservation using the website if you want.
You can check the returndate same as before if you collect the book before due time.If you collect the book with time you will get 14 more days to return the book.

05.What is fine?

Fine is applied when you are unable to return the issued book before due return date.Penalty will calculate until you return the the book.Rs.10 will fine will added every return day untill you return the issued book.You can check the penalty using website.
